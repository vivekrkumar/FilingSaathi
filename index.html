Your Partner in Business Registrations & Compliance

Filing Saathi simplifies the complexities of business registrations, tax, and compliance filings for startups and small to medium-sized enterprises in India.

The Problem: The Business Compliance Burden

Startups and small businesses face significant hurdles in navigating India's complex regulatory landscape, leading to a high rate of non-compliance and missed opportunities.

  • Opaque & Non-Intuitive Process: The entire process lacks transparency, leaving business owners with no clear traceability of their filings and causing a non-intuitive user experience.
  • Lack of Awareness on Government Schemes: Many startups and SMBs are unaware of government funding, grants, and subsidies, hindering financial inclusion and compliance with government mandates.
  • High Cost of Experts: Hiring a CA or lawyer for every compliance need is prohibitively expensive for early-stage and bootstrapped businesses.
  • Risk of Penalties: Mistakes or missed deadlines in filings can lead to significant government penalties, fines, and a damaged corporate reputation.

The Solution: The Filing Saathi Advantage

Filing Saathi is an AI-powered platform that provides a single, intuitive dashboard for all your business compliance needs, letting entrepreneurs focus on the core and give away the bore.

AI-Powered Guidance: Our smart assistant guides you step-by-step through company registrations and filings, ensuring accuracy and compliance.

Effortless Data Management: Securely import and organize all your financial documents in one place, streamlining annual filings and GST returns.

Affordable & Accessible: Our subscription model provides premium business compliance services at a fraction of the cost of traditional consultants.

Real-Time Compliance: Receive alerts and updates on new regulations and filing deadlines, so your business never misses a date again.

MVP & Beyond: Our Product Roadmap
